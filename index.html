#Insight 2k18

INSIGHT the technical fest hosted by the students of ccsit of Calicut university and department of computer science is a celebration of intelligence and knowledge combined to forge a new world of technology. It provides a platform for the students to explore and enhance their technical skills and also bestows opportunity to endeavour beyond the monotonous academic curriculum. The new edition of INSIGHT allure’s the upcoming generation with the perception of artificial intelligence and robotics. This jubilee puts forth a rostrum for 4000+ students from different colleges to conduct seminars, exhibitions, workshops, technical and cultural events. To this gathering we extend our warm welcome to participate and enjoy the events.

Every year INSIGHT is concentrating on a relevant area of the Information Technology. Therefore the programme will be conducted based on a particular theme. This year's theme for INSIGHT2k18 is AI & ROBOTICS.

Technova V3.0

Technova V3.0

The major attraction of INSIGHT is TECHNOVA. The aim of this event is to find out the best geek. It consist of 5 rounds namely Initum, Techsense, Intersepto, Extremist, and Jackpot. Topper of each round will be awarded with cash prizes.


RULES
  • There might be multiple levels for each round
  • Participants must bring Laptop
  • Participants are allowed to use the internet and other resources
  • Participation can be in group or individual
  • Each group can contain maximum 5 members
  • There will be points for each level
  • There will not be a knock out stage until 4th round
  • If the number of registration exceeds the limit, prelims may be conducted

ROUNDS
  • First round will be about basic logic
  • Second round is to test your cleverness and ability to use internet
  • Third round will test your investigation skills
  • Fourth round is meant for intermediate computer users
  • Fifth round is meant for advanced and intelligent computer users
