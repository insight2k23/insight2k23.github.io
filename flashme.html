Flash Me

The intention of the event is find out best photographer.


RULES
  • Participants should bring camera (mobile/camera)
  • The camera should be of minimum 5MP resolution.
  • Submit the photographs in .jpeg format
  • Theme will be given at the time of event
  • Each participant can submit up to two entries
  • No edited photos will be accepted for judgment
  • Entries should not contain name or any identity information of the participant
  • Entries submitted after the time limit or violating rules will be rejected
  • The decisions of the judges will be final
