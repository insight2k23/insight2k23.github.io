Webpreneur

The intention of the event is find out best web designers.


RULES
  • A team of maximum 2 participants will be allowed
  • Any number of teams can participate
  • There will be two rounds

GUIDELINES
Prelims
  • There will be 30 multiple choice questions to be completed within the time span of 20 mins
  • This round will be an elimination round
  • There will be negative marking
Final
  • The top 8 teams qualifying Prelims will compete in the final round
  • In the final round students are required to design a website on the given topic using the following tools:
    1. Photoshop
    2. Dreamweaver
    3. Front Page
